Handcrafted Upcycled Steel Spider Sculpture

Overview

One‑of‑a‑kind spider sculpture handcrafted from reclaimed steel and sealed for a lifetime of character.

  • Body forged from a bearing race and socket with a bent washer base—welded into a rugged arachnid form.
  • Eyes made from a chain link and nut; eight legs hand‑formed from steel wire.
  • Edges deburred and smoothed; finished with clear polyurethane to help resist rust while preserving the raw metal look.
  • Substantial weight—this desk guardian is solid steel.
  • Handmade in the USA.
